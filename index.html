Established 1984

Proudly serving the community since 1984, Sanjay Medical Store has been a familiar name in Karimuddinpur, Ghazipur (UP) — situated conveniently near the Post Office. Over four decades of trust, we believe that quality healthcare should be accessible, affordable, and friendly.

From everyday medicines to specialty prescriptions, baby care, wellness products and surgical essentials — we stock what your family needs, when you need it.

Our Journey

Four decades of care, since 1984

1984

The Beginning

Late Shri Balchand Maurya opens Sanjay Medical Store in Karimuddinpur with a single dream — affordable, genuine medicine for every family.

1995

A Trusted Name

Within a decade, the store becomes the go-to pharmacy for nearby villages — known for honest service and fair pricing.

2005

Expanding the Shelves

Inventory grows to include surgical essentials, baby care, ayurvedic, and wellness products — becoming a true family pharmacy.

2022

The Next Generation

Vivek Maurya takes the reins, continuing the family legacy with renewed dedication and modern care.

Today

Serving with the Same Heart
